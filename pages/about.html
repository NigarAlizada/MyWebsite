Namaste!

My name is Nigar, I am 26 years old and currently live in Baku, Azerbaijan. I graduated many years ago, got my Bachelor's degree in Information Systems and Technologies Engineering at ASAU. After university I decided to start working right away and since then I have worked and gained experience in different fields such as Administrative Assistance, Sales, Support Associate and etc. After years of wander I decided to find a job that will keep me happy and envolved for the rest of my life, my glance stopped on Web Development, in my opinion this path is perfect for me. I applied for scholarship grant held by our Ministry of Digital Development and Transport and won a place.I Studied Full-stack web development for a year there, but personally front-end is more appealing for me so I decided to keep that path. I studied HTML, CSS, JavaScript and ReactJS. Currently, I study TypeScript by myself. For me web-development is not about just developing apps, websites or products, but I believe that main goal of development is to develop a "solution" that will help people and one day make a difference.
